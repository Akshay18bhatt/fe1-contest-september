Mesmerizing Nainital

Nainital, "tal"(tal meaning lake) is a paradise for nature lovers. The most captivating aspect about Nainital is its hilly surroundings and the poised lakes alongwith that it receives heavy snowfall in winter and turns into a white wonderland, making it a popular Christmas and New Year destination.
